The Omnera mobile application is in development for iOS and Android. The complete product surface, including the feed, pre-trade intelligence, and cross-chain execution, will be available on mobile.

## Why Mobile Matters

Trading activity is not confined to desktop sessions. Market-moving events occur at all hours, and the interval between signal and action is often measured in seconds. A mobile-native feed means the behavioral model continues learning outside of desktop sessions. Every mobile interaction adds signal that refines the model, regardless of whether a trade executes.

## Design

The mobile experience is built around the same behavioral model as the desktop interface. The interaction pattern is scroll, evaluate, execute. All three actions occur within a single screen with no navigation between research and execution views.

The interface is optimized for single-hand use. Trade execution requires one tap. Pre-trade intelligence surfaces inline at the moment of decision.

## Notifications

The mobile application extends the feed beyond active sessions. When the behavioral model identifies a high-relevance opportunity, a notification is delivered. These notifications are generated by the same model that ranks the feed. They are not generic price alerts.

## Status

Both iOS and Android builds are in active development. A release date has not been announced. Waitlist members will be notified when the mobile application becomes available.
