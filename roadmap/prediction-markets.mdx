### Prediction Markets

A decentralized platform for trading on outcomes:

- **Token predictions**: Will a token hit a certain price?
- **Market events**: Will a protocol launch on time?
- **Ecosystem trends**: Which narrative will dominate?

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="AI Analysis">
    Our models continuously analyze market data to generate probability scores for various outcomes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Market Creation">
    Users can create prediction markets or trade on existing ones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trading">
    Buy and sell outcome shares based on your conviction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution">
    Markets resolve based on oracle-verified outcomes, and payouts are distributed automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## AI Model Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Real-time updates | Predictions update as new data comes in |
| Confidence scores | Clear indication of model certainty |
| Explanation | Understand why the AI made its prediction |
| Track record | Historical accuracy metrics for transparency |

<Warning>
  AI predictions are probabilistic estimates, not guarantees. Always do your own research and never trade more than you can afford to lose.
</Warning>
