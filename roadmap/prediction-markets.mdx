# Prediction Markets

A prediction market is a venue where participants take positions on the outcome of verifiable real-world events. Prices in a prediction market reflect the aggregate probability the market assigns to each outcome at any given time. A contract priced at 0.74 implies the market believes there is approximately a 74 percent probability the associated outcome will occur.

Prediction markets have been studied extensively in academic literature as mechanisms for aggregating dispersed information. Under conditions of sufficient liquidity and participant diversity, market prices have demonstrated forecasting accuracy that compares favorably to expert consensus and polling methodologies.

## Integration with the Feed

Omnera surfaces prediction market opportunities within the same feed as spot and perpetual futures positions. A user whose behavioral profile reflects interest in event-driven markets will see relevant prediction market contracts surfaced alongside other opportunities matched to their profile.

Positions are taken through the same one-tap execution interface used across all instrument types. There is no separate application or interface required.

## Settlement

Prediction market contracts settle to binary outcomes upon event resolution. A contract that resolves affirmatively settles at 1. A contract that resolves negatively settles at 0. Settlement is determined by the underlying venue based on verifiable real-world data sources.
