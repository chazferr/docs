## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Feed Surfaces Predictions">
    Prediction markets matching your behavioral profile appear in your feed alongside other opportunities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Provides Context">
    Each prediction comes with probability scores, reasoning, and confidence levels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You Trade">
    Take a position on the outcome you believe in. One tap.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resolution">
    Markets resolve based on verifiable outcomes. Winners get paid.
  </Step>
</Steps>
