## What Are Prediction Markets

Prediction markets are information markets that let users take positions on the outcome of real-world events. They reflect what participants collectively believe is likely to happen.

Examples of prediction market questions:
- Will a specific token reach a certain market cap by a given date?
- Which blockchain narrative will dominate this quarter?
- Will a protocol launch on schedule?

Prediction markets are not investments. They are markets for expressing a view on an outcome.
