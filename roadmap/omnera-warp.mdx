## Intent-Based Execution

As Omnera expands to more chains, execution becomes fully intent-based. You express what you want — the system figures out how.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Feed Surfaces Opportunity">
    An opportunity on any supported chain appears in your feed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="One Tap">
    Tap to execute. Omnera determines the optimal cross-chain route.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Solvers Compete">
    A decentralized solver network competes to find the best execution path. Competition = better rates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Atomic Execution">
    The trade executes atomically. Either it all happens, or none of it does.
  </Step>
</Steps>
