This roadmap reflects current development priorities. All timelines are subject to change.

## Personalized Feed

Live on desktop. The feed ranks opportunities by individual relevance across spot, perpetual futures, and prediction markets. Mobile is in development.

## Pre-Trade Intelligence

Live. On-chain, historical, and behavioral context surfaces inline at the point of decision. Additional data sources are being integrated.

## Cross-Chain Execution

Live on Solana and Base. Atomic settlement. No bridges. No wrapped tokens. Additional chains in progress.

## Prediction Markets

In development. Event-driven contracts will surface in the feed alongside spot and perpetual futures, ranked by relevance. Available where legally permitted. Users are responsible for compliance with applicable local laws. This is not financial advice.

## Mobile Application

In development for iOS and Android. Same behavioral model, optimized for one-hand use and one-tap execution.
