This roadmap reflects current development priorities. Features and timelines are subject to change.

## Product Status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Personalized Feed">
    Live on desktop. Mobile app in development. The feed learns from your behavior and surfaces relevant opportunities across spot, perps, and prediction markets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pre-Trade Intelligence">
    Live. Expanding signal coverage. Surfaces on-chain data, risk signals, and historical context before you trade.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cross-Chain Execution">
    Live on Solana and Base. More chains coming. Sub-500ms execution on physical infrastructure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Prediction Markets">
    In development. Information markets that let users take positions on the outcome of real-world events. Available where legally permitted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mobile App">
    In development. The full feed experience on iOS and Android.
  </Step>
</Steps>
