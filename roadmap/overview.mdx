This roadmap reflects current development priorities. All timelines are subject to change. Users who join during early access begin building behavioral signal ahead of each subsequent feature release.

## Personalized Feed

Live on desktop. The behavioral model is active from session one, capturing decision-level data across spot, perpetual futures, and prediction markets. Mobile experience is in development.

## Pre-Trade Intelligence

Live. Signal coverage currently includes on-chain wallet patterns, historical price behavior, and behavioral context from user feed profiles. Additional data sources are being integrated.

## Cross-Chain Execution

Live on Solana and Base. Execution is intent-based and atomic. Additional chain integrations are in progress. Each new chain expands both the opportunity set and the behavioral model.

## Prediction Markets

In development. Prediction market contracts will surface within the feed alongside spot and perpetual futures, ranked by individual relevance. Availability will be limited to jurisdictions where participation is legally permitted.

Prediction market features are available where legally permitted. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable local laws and regulations before participating. This is not financial advice.

## Mobile Application

In development for iOS and Android. The mobile interface is built around the same behavioral model as desktop, optimized for single-hand interaction and one-tap execution.
