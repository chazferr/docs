This roadmap reflects current development priorities. All timelines are subject to change. Users who join during early access begin building behavioral signal ahead of each subsequent feature release.

## Personalized Feed

**Live on desktop.** The behavioral model is active from session one. It captures decision-level data across spot, perpetual futures, and prediction markets. Every session refines the model. Mobile experience is in development.

## Pre-Trade Intelligence

**Live.** Signal coverage includes on-chain wallet patterns, historical price behavior, and behavioral context from user feed profiles. Additional data sources are being integrated on a rolling basis.

## Cross-Chain Execution

**Live on Solana and Base.** Execution is intent-based and atomic. The trade completes in full or does not execute. No bridges. No wrapped tokens. Additional chain integrations are in progress. Each new chain expands both the opportunity set and the behavioral model.

## Prediction Markets

**In development.** Prediction market contracts will surface within the feed alongside spot and perpetual futures, ranked by individual relevance. Availability will be limited to jurisdictions where participation is legally permitted.

Prediction market features are available where legally permitted. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable local laws and regulations before participating. This is not financial advice.

## Mobile Application

**In development for iOS and Android.** The mobile interface is built around the same behavioral model as desktop, optimized for single-hand interaction and one-tap execution. The model continues learning outside desktop sessions.

## Why Early Access Matters

Every feature that ships expands the behavioral model. Users who are active before each release have already accumulated the behavioral signal needed to benefit from it immediately. Users who join after launch start from zero.
