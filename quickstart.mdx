Omnera is in early access. Capacity is limited by design. Access is gated for quality, not exclusivity.

Access begins at [omnera.trade](https://omnera.io/contact). On receiving an invitation, you connect a non-custodial wallet supporting Solana or Base. Omnera does not custody funds. No private keys are requested. The wallet establishes your identity without granting access to assets.

The system begins learning from the first interaction. There is no configuration step. The advantage compounds from session one.

The feed, pre-trade intelligence, and cross-chain execution on Solana and Base are live. Execution-only fees. No subscription.
