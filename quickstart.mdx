# Get Started

Omnera is currently in early access. The following steps describe how to join and begin using the feed.

## Joining the Waitlist

Access begins at omnera.io. Users submit a waitlist request. Early access invitations are sent on a rolling basis as capacity expands.

## Connecting a Wallet

On receiving an invitation, users connect a non-custodial wallet. Omnera supports wallets across Solana and Base. No funds are held by Omnera at any point.

## The Feed

From the first session, the feed begins building a behavioral profile. It tracks what the user views, how long attention is held on a given opportunity, and what positions are ultimately taken. Each session refines the model.

## Early Access

Features available during early access may differ from the full product release. The feed, pre-trade intelligence, and cross-chain execution are available to early access users.
