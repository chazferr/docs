title: Get Started
description: Early access is capacity-limited. Here is how to get in.
---

Omnera is in early access. The system is capacity-limited by design. Every user's behavioral model requires dedicated compute, and model quality depends on controlled onboarding. Access is not gated for exclusivity. It is gated for signal quality.

Access begins at [omnera.trade](https://omnera.trade). Invitations are distributed on a rolling basis as infrastructure capacity expands. On receiving an invitation, you connect a non-custodial wallet supporting Solana or Base. Omnera does not custody funds at any point. No private keys are requested. The wallet establishes identity for the behavioral model without granting access to assets.

The behavioral model begins building from the first interaction. It captures what you view, how long your attention holds on each opportunity, which positions you consider but do not take, and what you scroll past. There is no configuration step. The model infers preferences from observed behavior and captures signal regardless of whether a trade executes.

The model on session five is materially different from session one. The model on session thirty bears little resemblance to session five. Behavioral data compounds. Early access users accumulate signal before the general population has access. Every session without a profile active is a session where discovery is identical to every other participant in the market.

The feed, pre-trade intelligence, and cross-chain execution on Solana and Base are available during early access. Trading fees are up to 1% at execution only. No subscription. Feature availability may change prior to general release.
