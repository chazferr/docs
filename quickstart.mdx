Omnera is in early access. The system is capacity-limited by design. Every user's behavioral model requires dedicated compute, and model quality depends on controlled onboarding. Access is not gated for exclusivity. It is gated for signal quality.

## Join the Waitlist

- Visit [omnera.trade](https://omnera.trade) to request access
- Invitations are distributed on a rolling basis as infrastructure capacity expands
- Waitlist position and community engagement both factor into selection

## Connect a Wallet

- On receiving an invitation, connect a non-custodial wallet supporting Solana or Base
- Omnera does not custody funds at any point. The wallet establishes identity for the behavioral model without granting access to assets
- No private keys are requested. No token approvals are required to begin

## First Session

- The behavioral model begins building from the first interaction with no configuration step
- It captures what the user views, how long attention holds on each opportunity, which positions are considered but not taken, and what is scrolled past
- These signals are captured regardless of whether a trade executes

## Why Starting Early Matters

The model on session five is materially different from session one. The model on session thirty bears little resemblance to session five.

- Behavioral data compounds. Each session adds signal that makes subsequent sessions more precise
- Early access users accumulate behavioral signal before the general population has access
- Every session without a profile active is a session where discovery is identical to every other participant in the market

## What Is Available

During early access, the following are live:

- Personalized feed across spot, perpetual futures, and prediction markets
- Pre-trade intelligence at the point of decision
- Cross-chain execution on Solana and Base
- Trading fees of up to 1% at execution only. No subscription. No platform fee

Feature availability may change prior to general release.
