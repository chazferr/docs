## What You Need

- A crypto wallet (MetaMask, OKX Wallet, Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
- SOL or ETH for trading

<Info>
  **Wallet Infrastructure**: Omnera uses [Turnkey](https://turnkey.com) for secure wallet infrastructure. Your keys remain protected with institutional-grade security.
</Info>

## Connect Your Wallet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Omnera">
    Launch the app and tap **Connect Wallet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Wallet">
    We support **MetaMask** and **OKX Wallet** (distribution partners), plus Phantom, Solflare, and other wallets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve">
    Approve the connection. Omnera can view your address but never access your funds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your First Session

Once connected, your feed starts learning immediately.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Scroll the Feed">
    Browse opportunities surfaced by Omnera. Spot, perps, meme coins, prediction markets — all in one place.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let It Learn">
    Every interaction teaches the feed. What you watch, what you scroll past, what catches your attention. No setup required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap for Context">
    Tap any opportunity to see pre-trade intelligence — contract safety, creator history, holder distribution, risk assessment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute">
    When you see something worth trading, execute in one tap. Cross-chain, MEV-protected, sub-500ms.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Trading involves significant risk. Never trade more than you can afford to lose.
</Warning>

## The Feed Gets Smarter

Your second session is better than your first. Your tenth is better than your fifth. The feed compounds — every session refines what gets surfaced to you.

What it tracks:
- What you watch closely
- What you scroll past
- What you almost traded
- What you executed
- Which chains and instruments you gravitate toward
