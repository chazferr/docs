## How to Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Join the Waitlist">
    Visit [omnera.io](https://omnera.io) and sign up for the waitlist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get Your Early Access Invite">
    Invites are rolling out in waves. Discord members get priority.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Wallet">
    Open Omnera and tap **Connect Wallet**. We support MetaMask, OKX Wallet, Phantom, Solflare, and other popular wallets. Omnera can view your address but never access your funds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your Feed Starts Learning">
    From your first session, the feed begins learning. What you look at. What you scroll past. What catches your attention. No setup required. It gets more relevant every time you use it.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  **Wallet Infrastructure**: Omnera uses [Turnkey](https://turnkey.com) for secure wallet infrastructure. Your keys remain protected with institutional-grade security.
</Info>
