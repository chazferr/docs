Omnera is in early access. The system is capacity-limited by design. Every behavioral model requires dedicated compute, and model quality depends on controlled onboarding. Access is gated for signal quality, not exclusivity.

Access begins at [omnera.trade](https://omnera.trade). On receiving an invitation, you connect a non-custodial wallet supporting Solana or Base. Omnera does not custody funds. No private keys are requested. The wallet establishes identity for the behavioral model without granting access to assets.

The model begins building from the first interaction. It captures decision-level behavioral data regardless of whether a trade executes. There is no configuration step. The model on session five is materially different from session one. The model on session thirty bears little resemblance to session five.

The feed, pre-trade intelligence, and cross-chain execution on Solana and Base are available during early access. Trading fees are up to 1% at execution only. No subscription.
