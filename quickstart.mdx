Omnera is currently in private beta. Access is granted through the waitlist on a rolling basis.

## What to Expect

When your access is activated, you will connect a wallet and immediately begin building a behavioral profile. The system does not require configuration. It observes how you interact with the market — what you open, what you skip, what you revisit — and begins adapting the discovery surface to your patterns within the first session.

There is no setup wizard. There is no preference form. The feed calibrates itself.

## What You'll Need

- **A Solana-compatible wallet.** Phantom, Backpack, or any standard Solana wallet.
- **SOL for gas.** A small amount of SOL to cover transaction fees on-chain.
- **No KYC required.** Omnera is self-custodial. Your keys, your funds, your data.

## First Session

Once inside, the experience is immediate:

1. **Connect your wallet.** One click. No account creation.
2. **Open the feed.** The discovery surface loads with market activity. On your first session, the feed reflects broad market signal. By your second or third session, it begins to narrow.
3. **Trade.** When you see something worth executing, the system routes your intent to the optimal venue — spot, perps, or prediction markets — and settles atomically across chains.

Your profile compounds. Every session sharpens the model. The longer you use Omnera, the more divergent your feed becomes from everyone else's.

<Note>
  Omnera never stores your private keys. All execution is self-custodial. The behavioral model is tied to your wallet address, not to any personal identity.
</Note>
