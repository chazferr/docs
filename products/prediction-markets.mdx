title: Prediction Markets
description: Event-driven contracts surfaced by behavioral relevance.
---

Prediction markets let you take positions on the outcome of real-world events. They reveal dimensions of decision-making that spot and futures cannot: how you evaluate uncertainty, how you size conviction, how you think about time.

Omnera surfaces prediction market contracts in the same feed as spot and perpetual futures. If your history reflects interest in event-driven markets, relevant contracts appear alongside everything else. One interface. One-tap execution where permitted.

Prediction market features are available where legally permitted. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable local laws and regulations before participating. This is not financial advice.
