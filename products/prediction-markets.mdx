title: Prediction Markets
description: Event-driven contracts surfaced by behavioral relevance.
---

Prediction markets let you take positions on verifiable real-world events. They reveal how you process uncertainty, time horizon, and conviction in ways that spot and futures trading cannot. A platform without this signal sees an incomplete version of how you think.

Omnera surfaces prediction market contracts in the same feed as spot and perpetual futures, ranked by individual relevance. If your behavioral profile reflects interest in event-driven markets, relevant contracts appear alongside everything else. One interface. One-tap execution where permitted.

Each prediction market interaction refines the behavioral model in ways that other instruments cannot replicate. The result is a more complete profile and a more precise feed.

Prediction market features are available where legally permitted. Users are responsible for ensuring compliance with applicable local laws and regulations before participating. This is not financial advice.
