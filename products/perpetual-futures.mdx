Most platforms isolate perpetual futures from spot and other instruments. You navigate to a separate section, assess funding manually, and execute without context from the rest of your activity.

Omnera does not separate instruments by type. Perps appear in the same feed as spot and prediction markets, surfaced when they are contextually relevant to you. Execution is cross-chain and atomic.

Perpetual futures involve leverage and carry significant risk of loss. Positions can be liquidated if margin requirements are not maintained. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose. Omnera does not provide trading advice. All execution decisions are made by the user.
