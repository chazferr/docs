Perpetual futures are the dominant instrument for directional exposure in crypto. Most platforms present them in isolation from spot and other instruments. You navigate to a separate section, assess funding and basis manually, and execute without context from the rest of your activity. The decision is fragmented before it begins.

Omnera surfaces perpetual futures alongside spot and prediction markets in the same feed, ranked by behavioral relevance. The model captures how you engage with leveraged instruments and uses that signal to refine what surfaces next. Perps do not live on a separate page. They appear where they are relevant, when they are relevant.

Execution is cross-chain and atomic. Fees are up to 1% at execution only.

Perpetual futures involve leverage and carry significant risk of loss. Positions can be liquidated if margin falls below required maintenance levels. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose. Omnera does not provide trading advice. All execution decisions are made by the user.
