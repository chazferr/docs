Transaction logs record what you executed. They do not record what you considered, what held your attention, what you almost acted on, or what you returned to three sessions later. Every trading platform is blind to this data. Omnera is not.

Your feed is ranked by what matters to you, not what is trending. Two traders opening the feed at the same moment see different content in a different order because their histories diverge. The feed operates across spot, perpetual futures, and prediction markets on Solana and Base in a single interface.

The advantage compounds from session one. A trader on session fifty operates on a qualitatively different surface than a trader on session three.

The feed is not a feature. It is a compounding advantage that begins the moment a profile exists.
