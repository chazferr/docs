title: Personalized Feed
description: Discovery ranked by individual relevance, not aggregate popularity.
---

Every trading platform ranks assets the same way. Volume. Price change. Market cap. The list is identical for every user. A professional trader and a first-time buyer see the same screen because the platform has no mechanism to tell them apart.

The data to distinguish them exists. No platform captures it. Transaction logs record what you executed. They do not record what you considered, what held your attention, what you almost acted on, or what you returned to three sessions later. Omnera captures this decision layer and uses it to rank the feed.

Every opportunity receives a relevance score derived from a behavioral model that updates each session. The model operates across spot, perpetual futures, and prediction markets on Solana and Base. Two traders opening the feed at the same moment see different content in a different order. Behavioral history determines rank, not aggregate volume.

The model compounds. Early sessions produce a coarse signal. Later sessions resolve finer distinctions that match your actual pattern. A trader on session fifty operates on a qualitatively different information surface than a trader on session three. Each session sharpens every session that follows.

The feed is not a feature. It is a compounding advantage that begins the moment a profile exists.
