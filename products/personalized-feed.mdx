## How It Learns

<Steps>
  <Step title="It Watches Everything">
    Not just your trades. The feed tracks what you look at, how long you look, what you skip, and what you almost execute. This is behavioral data that most platforms never capture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It Builds Your Profile">
    Over time, the feed develops a model of your trading behavior. What market caps attract you. Which risk profiles match your style. What instruments you gravitate toward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It Surfaces Relevant Opportunities">
    The feed stops showing you generic trending lists. It starts showing you opportunities that match your actual behavior. Not what's popular. What's relevant to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It Compounds">
    Each session makes the next one smarter. Each new instrument and chain adds to the profile. The longer you use it, the more refined it gets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What It Tracks

Most platforms only track what you bought and sold. Omnera captures the full decision funnel.

| Signal | What It Tells Us |
|--------|------------------|
| What you watched closely | Active interest |
| What you scrolled past | Not relevant to your style |
| What you almost traded | Hesitation patterns |
| What you executed | Confirmed preferences |
| Time spent on an opportunity | Depth of consideration |
| Chains and instruments used | Infrastructure preferences |

This behavioral layer is what makes the feed personal. No one else has your profile.

## Feed vs. Search

Most platforms require you to search for what you want. You need to already know what you're looking for.

The feed works differently. Opportunities come to you, matched to your behavior.

| | Search-Based Platforms | Omnera Feed |
|---|------------------------|-------------|
| Discovery | You search | Feed surfaces |
| Personalization | None | Learns every session |
| Instruments | One type per app | Spot, perps, prediction markets |
| Chains | One chain per app | Solana + Base, expanding |
| Context | You research separately | Pre-trade intelligence included |
