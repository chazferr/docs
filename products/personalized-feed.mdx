## How It Learns

<Steps>
  <Step title="It Watches Everything">
    Not just your trades. The feed tracks what you look at, how long you look, what you skip, and what you almost execute. This is behavioral data most platforms never capture.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It Builds Your Profile">
    Over time, the feed develops a model of your trading behavior. What market caps attract you. Which risk profiles match your style. What instruments you gravitate toward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It Surfaces Your Opportunities">
    The feed stops showing you generic trending lists and starts showing you opportunities that match your actual behavior. Not what's popular. What's relevant to you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="It Compounds">
    Each session makes the next one smarter. Each new instrument and chain compounds the profile. The longer you use it, the sharper it gets.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Makes This Different

Jupiter sees what you traded. **Omnera knows what you almost traded.**

Most platforms only track execution data — what you bought, what you sold, at what price. Omnera captures the full decision funnel:

| Signal | What It Tells Us |
|--------|------------------|
| What you watched closely | Active interest |
| What you scrolled past | Not relevant to your style |
| What you almost traded | Hesitation patterns |
| What you executed | Confirmed preferences |
| Time spent on an opportunity | Depth of consideration |
| Chains and instruments used | Infrastructure preferences |

This behavioral layer is what makes the feed personal. No one else has your profile.

## Feed vs. Search

Traditional platforms make you search for opportunities. You need to already know what you're looking for.

The feed inverts this. Opportunities come to you, matched to your behavior.

| | Traditional | Omnera Feed |
|---|-------------|-------------|
| Discovery | You search | Feed surfaces |
| Personalization | None | Every session |
| Instruments | One type per app | Spot, perps, prediction markets |
| Chains | One chain per app | Solana + Base, expanding |
| Context | You research | Pre-trade intelligence included |
