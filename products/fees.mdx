---
title: Fee Structure
description: Execution-aligned. No subscription. No platform fee.
---

Most platforms charge for access. Monthly subscriptions. Tiered memberships. Premium data feeds. This model creates an incentive to retain subscribers regardless of whether the platform delivers value on any given trade. The platform is compensated for attention, not outcomes.

Omnera charges fees only when a trade executes. If you do not trade, you pay nothing. All trades across supported instruments and chains are subject to a fee of up to 1%, regardless of instrument type, chain, or account tier. Failed or reverted executions are not charged.

The feed, the behavioral model, pre-trade intelligence, and wallet connection cost nothing. The entire discovery and evaluation experience is free. Revenue is generated only at the moment you decide to act.

When a platform charges for access, its incentive is retention. When a platform charges at execution, its incentive is to surface opportunities worth executing. Omnera is compensated under one condition: a trade you chose to make.
