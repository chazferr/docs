Most platforms charge for access: monthly subscriptions, tiered memberships, premium data feeds. This model creates an incentive to retain subscribers regardless of whether the platform delivers value on any given trade.

Omnera charges fees only when a trade executes. If the user does not trade, the user pays nothing.

## How Fees Work

- All trades across supported instruments and chains are subject to a fee of up to 1%
- The fee applies at execution regardless of instrument type, chain, or account tier
- There is no subscription. No monthly charge. No platform fee
- Failed or reverted executions are not charged

## What Costs Nothing

- The personalized feed
- The behavioral model
- Pre-trade intelligence
- Wallet connection
- Access to all supported instruments and chains

The entire discovery and evaluation experience is free. Revenue is generated only at the point of execution.

## Why This Alignment Matters

When a platform charges for access, its incentive is retention. When a platform charges at execution, its incentive is to surface opportunities worth executing.

- Omnera has no financial incentive to push unnecessary trades
- The system is compensated only when a user decides, on their own terms, to act
- The fee model ensures that the platform and the user succeed under the same condition: a trade the user chose to make
