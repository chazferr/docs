---
title: Fee Structure
description: Execution-aligned. No subscription. No platform fee.
---

Most platforms charge for access. Monthly subscriptions. Tiered memberships. Premium data feeds. The incentive is to retain subscribers regardless of whether the platform delivers value on any given trade.

Omnera charges fees only when a trade executes. If you do not trade, you pay nothing. All trades are subject to a fee of up to 1%, regardless of instrument, chain, or account tier. Failed executions are not charged.

The feed, the behavioral model, pre-trade intelligence, and wallet connection cost nothing. Revenue is generated only at the moment you decide to act.

Omnera is compensated under one condition: a trade you chose to make.
