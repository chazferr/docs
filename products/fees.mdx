# Fee Structure

Omnera charges fees only at the point of execution. There are no subscription fees, no platform access fees, and no fees for using the feed or pre-trade intelligence.

## Trading Fees

All trades across all supported instruments and chains are subject to a fee of up to 1%. Users are only charged when a trade executes.

## What Is Not Charged

The personalized feed carries no fee. Pre-trade intelligence carries no fee. Wallet connection and account creation carry no fee.
