---
title: Fee Structure
description: What Omnera charges and when.
---

Omnera charges fees at the point of execution only. There are no subscription fees, platform access fees, or charges for using the feed or pre-trade intelligence.

## Trading Fees

All trades across supported instruments and chains are subject to a fee of up to 1%. This fee applies uniformly regardless of instrument type or chain.

## What Is Not Charged

The personalized feed, pre-trade intelligence, wallet connection, and account creation carry no fee. The only cost a user incurs is the execution fee at the time of trade.
