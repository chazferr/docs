title: Fee Structure
description: Execution-aligned. No subscription. No platform fee.
---

Omnera's fee model is execution-aligned. Fees are charged only when a trade executes. There is no subscription, no platform fee, and no charge for accessing the feed or pre-trade intelligence.

## Trading Fees

All trades across supported instruments and chains are subject to a fee of up to 1%. The fee is the same regardless of instrument type, chain, or account tier.

## Alignment

The fee structure ensures Omnera generates revenue only when a user takes action. The feed, the behavioral model, pre-trade intelligence, and wallet connection cost the user nothing. The system has no incentive to push unnecessary trades. It is compensated only when a user decides, on their own terms, to execute.
