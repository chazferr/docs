Execution-only fee. No subscription. No platform fee.

Omnera charges nothing for the feed, the intelligence layer, or wallet connection. The entire discovery and evaluation experience is free. Revenue is generated only when a trade executes.

All trades are subject to a fee of up to 1%, regardless of instrument, chain, or account tier. Failed executions are not charged.

Omnera is compensated under one condition: a trade you chose to make.
