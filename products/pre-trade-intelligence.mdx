## Built Into the Feed

Pre-trade intelligence is not a separate product. It is embedded directly in the feed flow.

1. You see an opportunity in your feed
2. You tap it
3. Context loads in seconds
4. You review the information
5. You decide whether to execute

No tab switching. No context loss. The information appears when you need it. Before you trade.

Your transaction does not enter the public mempool. This reduces the risk of front-running and sandwich attacks.
