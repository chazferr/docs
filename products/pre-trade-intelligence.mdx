# Pre-Trade Intelligence

Context at the moment of decision.

Execution without context is speculation on incomplete information. The standard trading interface presents price, volume, and order book depth at the moment a user is deciding whether to enter a position. These inputs describe current market state. They do not describe whether current market state is anomalous, whether similar conditions have historically preceded reversals, or whether the asset exhibits behavioral patterns that are relevant to the user's observed trading style.

Omnera surfaces this context before execution occurs.

## Signal Layer

At the point of decision, Omnera presents a synthesis of on-chain behavioral data and historical price pattern analysis for the opportunity in view. This includes recent wallet activity patterns among significant holders, historical volatility distribution across comparable market conditions, and any anomalous on-chain signals that diverge from the asset's baseline behavior.

The signal layer does not generate a recommendation. It presents structured information that a trader would otherwise need to source from multiple external tools, manually, before the opportunity window closes.

## Informational Scope

Pre-trade intelligence covers three categories of signal. On-chain data reflects observable blockchain activity associated with the asset. Historical pattern data reflects how the asset has behaved under comparable market conditions in the past. Behavioral context reflects how the current opportunity compares to opportunities the user has previously engaged with, based on their feed profile.

No signal category constitutes a prediction of future price movement. Markets are non-stationary. Historical patterns are descriptive, not prescriptive.

## Disclaimer

Pre-trade intelligence is informational only. It does not constitute financial advice. Past market behavior does not guarantee future results. All trading decisions are made solely by the user.
