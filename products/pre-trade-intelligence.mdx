title: Pre-Trade Intelligence
description: Structured context at the point of decision.
---

Evaluating an on-chain opportunity typically requires opening multiple tools, cross-referencing data manually, and synthesizing information from sources that were not designed to work together. By the time the evaluation is complete, the opportunity has repriced or you have entered on incomplete information. The bottleneck is not skill. It is the time cost of assembling context from fragmented sources.

Omnera eliminates the assembly step. When you engage with an opportunity in the feed, structured context surfaces automatically. On-chain data includes wallet activity among the largest holders, transaction patterns that diverge from baseline, and contract-level risk indicators including mint authority status, liquidity lock state, and holder concentration. Historical data describes how the asset has behaved under comparable conditions in prior periods. Behavioral context compares the opportunity against your own feed history and prior engagement with similar setups.

The intelligence does not surface before the decision or after it. It surfaces at the exact moment your attention is on a specific opportunity. There is no separate research dashboard. No tab to switch to. Context exists inline, within the feed, at the moment it is relevant. Every second spent navigating to an external tool is a second the market is moving.

The system does not generate trade recommendations. It does not predict price movement. It does not assign buy or sell signals. Markets are non-stationary. Historical patterns are descriptive, not prescriptive. The signal layer provides context. You make the decision.

Pre-trade intelligence is informational only. It does not constitute financial advice. Past market behavior does not guarantee future results. All trading decisions are made solely by the user.
