# Cross-Chain Execution

One interface. Every chain. No bridges.

Cross-chain trading has historically required users to manage multiple steps across multiple interfaces. Find a bridge. Wait for confirmation. Receive a wrapped asset. Swap again. Pay fees at each step. The process is slow, error-prone, and pulls attention away from the trade itself.

Omnera removes this entirely. When the feed surfaces an opportunity on any supported chain, the user expresses intent. Omnera resolves the optimal execution path automatically. There is no bridging step. There are no wrapped tokens. The user receives native assets on the destination chain.

This is not a bridge. Omnera does not custody assets during transit. Execution is atomic. Either the full trade completes or nothing happens. There is no partial state.

## Supported Chains

Omnera currently supports Solana and Base. Additional chains are in development and will be added to the feed as they come online.

## Perpetual Futures

Omnera surfaces perpetual futures contracts across supported venues. A perpetual futures contract allows a user to take a leveraged long or short position on a crypto asset without an expiry date. The position can be held indefinitely, subject to funding rates and margin requirements.

Perpetual futures involve leverage and carry significant risk of loss. A position can be liquidated if margin falls below the required maintenance threshold. Only trade with capital you can afford to lose. Omnera does not provide trading advice. All execution decisions are made by the user.

## Fees

Omnera charges up to 0.5% on Solana trades. Cross-chain execution carries a spread of up to 0.38%. There are no subscriptions and no hidden fees. Users pay only when they trade.
