## The Problem

Your funds are on Solana. The opportunity is on Base. To act on it, you typically need to:

1. Find a bridge
2. Wait for the bridge transaction
3. Receive wrapped tokens
4. Swap wrapped tokens for native assets
5. Make your trade
6. Pay fees at every step

That process can take 30 minutes or more across multiple transactions.
