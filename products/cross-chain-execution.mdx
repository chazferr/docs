A trader holding SOL who identifies an opportunity on Base faces a sequence. Bridge, wait, receive wrapped tokens, swap, execute. Each step adds latency, cost, and failure risk. By the time the sequence completes, the opportunity has moved. This friction filters out every cross-chain trade that requires speed.

Omnera removes the sequence. You express intent. The system resolves the optimal path. Execution is atomic: the trade completes in full or does not execute at all. No wrapped tokens. No custody during transit. Native assets arrive on the destination chain.

Solana and Base are live. Additional chains are in development. As each chain is integrated, opportunities surface in the feed automatically.

Your job is to decide. Everything after the decision is infrastructure.
