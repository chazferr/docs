A trader holding SOL who identifies an opportunity on Base faces a sequence of operations: find a bridge, wait for confirmation, receive wrapped tokens, swap into native assets, then execute. Each step introduces latency, cost, and failure risk. By the time the sequence completes, the opportunity has repriced or disappeared. This friction is structural, and it filters out every cross-chain opportunity that requires speed.

Omnera removes the sequence entirely. The user expresses intent. The system resolves the execution path. Native assets arrive on the destination chain.

## Execution Model

Omnera uses intent-based routing. When a trade is initiated, the system evaluates available paths across supported chains and selects the optimal route. Execution is atomic: the trade completes in full or does not execute at all. There is no partial state, no wrapped tokens, and no funds held in custody during transit.

This is not a bridge. Bridges custody assets during transfer, creating a class of risk that has historically resulted in billions of dollars in losses across the industry. Atomic execution eliminates this custody window entirely.

## Supported Chains

Solana and Base are live. Additional chains are in active development. As each chain is integrated, opportunities on that chain surface in the feed automatically. Each new chain also expands the behavioral model, because cross-chain activity reveals patterns that single-chain data cannot.
